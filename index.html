About us

At Loving Homes, we offer a luxury dog hotel in the heart of Hong Kong, providing a safe and comfortable environment that ensures every guest enjoys care and happiness. We provide a full range of modern facilities, including a grooming parlour and spa, rooms with underfloor heating, cooling, and TV, a chauffeur ‘collect and return’ service, secure outdoor and indoor play areas, and 24-hour on-call veterinary care. We also offer personalized packages such as Premium, Classic, and Day, which include daily walks, fun activities, and special care tailored to each dog’s needs.
